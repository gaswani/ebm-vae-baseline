## Data governance

- Raw operational/production EBM data is **not** distributed with this repository.
- Any datasets used with this codebase should be handled according to applicable RRA data policies and confidentiality requirements.
- If you add example/sample data, ensure it is **synthetic** or **properly anonymized** and approved for sharing.
